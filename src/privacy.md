# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 1, 2025

**Last Updated:** February 5, 2026

DownBad is a service owned and operated by **Future Vision Concepts, LLC**, an Ohio Limited Liability Company ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our uptime monitoring service at downbad.app (the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. As fellow indie developers, we understand the importance of data privacy and have designed our Service to collect only what's necessary to provide you with reliable monitoring.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email address** (required) — Used for authentication, notifications, and account communications
- **Display name** (optional) — Used to personalize your experience
- **Avatar URL** (optional) — Used for profile display

### 1.2 Monitor Configuration Data

When you set up monitors, we collect the configuration details you provide:

- URLs, domains, IP addresses, and ports you wish to monitor
- Monitor names and descriptions
- Check frequency and scheduling preferences
- HTTP methods, headers, and expected responses
- SSL certificate, DNS, and WHOIS monitoring parameters
- Notification preferences and alert thresholds

### 1.3 Monitor Results and Performance Data

When our Service checks your monitored endpoints, we collect:

- Success or failure status of each check
- Response times and latency measurements
- HTTP status codes and response headers
- Error messages and diagnostic information
- Timestamps of all monitoring activities
- Geographic region from which checks were performed

### 1.4 Notification and Delivery Data

To deliver alerts and maintain service quality, we collect:

- Email addresses of notification recipients you configure
- Webhook URLs and configurations you provide
- Notification delivery status and timestamps
- Delivery failures and error details (for troubleshooting)

### 1.5 Audit and Security Logs

For security and compliance purposes, we automatically collect:

- IP addresses used to access your account
- Browser and device information (user agent)
- Actions performed within your account
- Timestamps of account activities

### 1.6 Local Storage and Preference Data

To personalize your experience and ensure the site renders correctly, we use functional cookies and browser localStorage to store:

- UI theme selections and customizations
- Display preferences and layout settings
- Other temporary interface state necessary for site functionality

For authenticated users, your preferences are also stored in our database so they sync across devices. For unauthenticated visitors, preferences are stored only in your browser via functional cookies.

Functional cookies are strictly necessary for the site to operate correctly and do not require consent under GDPR or ePrivacy regulations.

### 1.7 Anonymous Usage Analytics

To understand how people use our Service, we collect anonymous, aggregate usage data using our own self-hosted analytics system. This includes:

- **Page views** — Which pages are visited and in what order
- **Click events** — Which elements and features are interacted with
- **Referral source** — How you arrived at our site (e.g., a link from another website)
- **General device information** — Browser type, screen size, and operating system (collected in aggregate, not used to identify individuals)

**What we do NOT collect through analytics:**

- IP addresses (not stored or logged)
- Personal identifiers or account information
- Cross-site browsing behavior
- Device fingerprints

Our analytics system uses **no cookies** and **no third-party services**. All analytics data is processed and stored entirely on our own infrastructure. Analytics data is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to identify any individual user.

## 9. Cookies, Local Storage, and Analytics

### 9.1 Cookies We Use

We use a minimal set of cookies, all of which are strictly functional:

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Required |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| **Authentication cookies** | Maintain your logged-in session | Yes |
| **Functional cookies** | Store display preferences (theme, UI settings) for unauthenticated visitors to ensure the site renders correctly | Yes |

All cookies we use are first-party (set by downbad.app) and strictly necessary for the site to function. **No consent banner is required** for these cookies under GDPR and ePrivacy regulations.

### 9.2 Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to persist UI preferences on your device. For authenticated users, preferences are synced to your account so they carry across devices.

### 9.3 Self-Hosted Anonymous Analytics

We operate our own analytics system to understand usage patterns in aggregate. Our analytics:

- **Do not use cookies** of any kind
- **Do not store IP addresses**
- **Do not track users across sites**
- **Do not use third-party services** — all data stays on our infrastructure
- **Cannot identify individual users** — data is anonymous and aggregate only

We collect page views, click events, referral sources, and general device information (browser type, screen size) solely to improve the Service. This data is never sold, shared, or used for advertising.

### 9.4 What We Do NOT Use

We do **not** use:

- Third-party analytics services (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no Mixpanel)
- Advertising or retargeting cookies
- Cross-site tracking technologies
- Device fingerprinting
- Any cookie or tracking mechanism that requires user consent

### 9.5 Managing Browser Storage

You can clear cookies and localStorage through your browser settings. Note that clearing authentication cookies will require you to log in again, and clearing functional cookies or localStorage may reset your display preferences.
